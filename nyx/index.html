Nyx

Passive: Enemies are 20% less accurate when targeting Nyx, and when Absorb is not active.

1st Ability, Mind Control: Spend 25 energy to mind control one target up to 60m away for 45 seconds. Any damage dealt to the target in the first 4 seconds is converted into a damage buff. <graph>

2nd Ability, Psychic Bolts: Spend 50 energy to throw 6 bolts 60m away for 45 seconds. Any damage dealt to the target in the first 4 seconds is converted into a damage buff.
