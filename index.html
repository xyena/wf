Home Page

This site was created to promote my own off-meta builds, fashionframe, tips, and tomfoolery. This site is not accepting user-submitted content. If you have constructive criticism for the webmaster (me), concerning site layout, bugs, etc., please refer to the email at the bottom of the page.

This site will use an informal tone every now and again throughout its guides. It is the author's belief that a relaxed tone better conveys the importance and emphasis on elements that are deserving of such.

For the sake of simplicity, all builds will assume a Max Rank frame. I see no point in listing what an ability does when unranked, it just clutters the page.
