Home Page

This site was created as an ad-free and lightweight alternative to the Fandom-hosted ("Wiki") site. It contains detailed descriptions of a Warframe's pros and cons as well as various build options which may be off-meta. The options provided should assist players who have acquired a Frame that they must use, yet dislike the use of it in its generally accepted role.

This site is NOT accepting user-submitted content. If you have constructive criticism for the webmaster (me), concerning site layout, bugs, etc., please refer to the email at the bottom of the page.

This site will use an informal tone every now and again throughout its guides. It is the author's belief that a relaxed tone better conveys the importance and emphasis on elements that are deserving of such.

For the sake of simplicity, all Warframe ability detail will assume a Max Rank frame. The use cases for otherwise are few and far between.
